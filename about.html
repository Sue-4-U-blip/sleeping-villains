A Little About Us

Sleeping Villains is a three piece from Chicago, IL. Playing power pop songs to dance and cry your gay heart to like no one's around. From DIY spots, to established venues; We have been all over the Chicago area and surrounding areas with our high-energy style and songs that covers the whole scope of mood, love, life, and murder.

We are comprised of lead singer Taylor Farina, Basssit Sue Calarco, and Drummer powerhouse Rachel Villa. After meeting in 2019, we have gone onto release 2 EPs, countless singles, and a few cute photos of us. (Also Our sick merch that is designed in-house!)
